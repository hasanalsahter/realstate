The Queen Central Park project was realized by Simpaş REIC in Bomonti, Şişli. The project will consist of 1080 residences with a total area of 26 thousand and consists of 1 + 0, 1 + 1, 2 + 1, 2,5 + 1 and 3,5 + 1 flats. In the Queen Central Park project, the modular system can be used to create living areas in a single unit or in different square meters where 4 units can be combined if desired.
