Zorlu Center, which is built on the Zincirlikuyu highway land, which is shown as the most valuable location of Istanbul, consists of 4 blocks and 584 houses with 18 and 22 floors in total. Raffles Istanbul Hotel is located in one block of the project, while the other three blocks have tower residences and terrace houses. The apartments in the tower residence block have a large balcony.
